**Community Day is here!**

Join penguins from across Antarctica for a day of celebration, connection, and community building.

**What to expect:**

- **Morning**: Community breakfast and welcome ceremony
- **Midday**: Team-building activities and ice games
- **Afternoon**: Storytelling sessions and cultural exchange
- **Evening**: Grand feast and dance party under the aurora

This is a day to celebrate our diverse penguin community, share stories, and strengthen the bonds that make us stronger together.

All penguins welcome. Families encouraged to attend. Free fish snacks provided!
